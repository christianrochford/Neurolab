Getting here

Our labs and offices are located at Queen Square 33, 4th floor.

Our imaging studies (using functional MRI and magnetoencephalography) are conducted at the Wellcome Trust Centre for Neuroimaging (FIL), which is just across the Square.

Queen Square is halfway between the Holborn (Piccadilly and Central Lines) and Russell Square (Piccadilly Line) Tube stations.

This is central London, so best not to come by car. The traffic wardens are notoriously efficient. And traffic often awful.

Queen Square is roughly half a mile south of St. Pancras, Kings Cross and Euston Stations. From Kings Cross, Russell Square underground is one stop on the Piccadilly Line. Euston Station it is a short walk down Woburn Place.

London Heathrow is on the Piccadilly Line, journey time one hour. Gatwick Airport has a fast link with London Victoria Station, journey time 30-35 mins and London Stansted has a fast connection to London Liverpool Street Station, journey time 45 mins. London City Airport is about 40 minutes from here.
